Welcome to Tanquery's

Located in the historic Metcalf Building, Tanqueray's has been serving downtown Orlando cocktails, live music and good vibes for decades. Come see us between 5PM and 8PM on weekdays where happy hour will get you 50% off your bar tab, or visit us later where live music is played every night starting at 10:30PM. Come grab a drink and say hello. We can't wait to see you!

  • Happy Hour M-F (5-8PM)

  • Good Vibes
